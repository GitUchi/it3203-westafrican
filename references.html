References & Resources

A curated list of books, websites, and academic resources consulted during the research for this project.

Books

Osseo-Asare, F. (2005). Food Culture in Sub-Saharan Africa. Greenwood Press.

A comprehensive academic overview of food traditions across sub-Saharan Africa, including detailed chapters on West African staples and culinary practices.

Harris, J. B. (1998). The Africa Cookbook: Tastes of a Continent. Simon & Schuster.

An award-winning cookbook covering traditional recipes from across Africa, with significant coverage of West African cuisine and its diaspora connections.

Albala, K. (Ed.). (2011). Food Cultures of the World Encyclopedia. Greenwood.

Contains detailed entries on the food cultures of individual West African nations including Nigeria, Ghana, Senegal, and Mali.
